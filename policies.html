Our Policies


Returns

We will accept returns with original reciept up to 60 days from purchase date. All returns are refunded to the original payment method. Credit card returns can take up to 48 hours to clear. Cash and check returns process same-day. If it is after 60 days, you may contact us and we will exchange your product with either another one we have in stock or store credit to be used within one year.

Shipping

We offer international shipping on all of our products. Shipping is calculated based on available rates on the day your order is placed. For up-to-date rates Contact Us.

Financing

Durham House of Pets does not offer any financing options at this time. We do accept dicover, mastercard, and visa.
